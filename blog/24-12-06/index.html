The Invisible World

I

'm becoming preoccupied once again with the INvisible World. It's been popping up again, here and there.

These carols sung to cheer my passage through the world I see, For completion I dedicate to the Invisible World. -- Walt Whitman
Only through prayer can we turn from the visible to the invisible. -- Andrei Tarkovsky's Andrei Rublev, 1966

What is the invisible world? I remember when I was in college, I was obsessed with spiritual enlightenment; with understanding that which undergirds all things. After a long time, and many seismic interior shake-ups, the interest is returning. How do we turn from the world of appearances, to the world that we cannot see? What, exactly, am I talking about?

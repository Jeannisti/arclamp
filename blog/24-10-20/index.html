Called to Love

BigCap

Be you therefore perfect, as also your heavenly Father is perfect. - Matthew 5:48

Greater love than this no man hath, that a man lay down his life for his friends. - John 15:13

Master, which is the greatest commandment in the law? Jesus said to him: Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with thy whole heart, and with thy whole soul, and with thy whole mind. This is the greatest and the first commandment. And the second is like to this: Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself. On these two commandments dependeth the whole law and the prophets. - Matthew 22:36-40

A new commandment I give unto you: That you love one another, as I have loved you, that you also love one another. By this shall all men know that you are my disciples, if you have love one for another. - John 13:34

And if I should distribute all my goods to feed the poor, and if I should deliver my body to be burned, and have not charity, it profiteth me nothing. - 1 Corinthians 13:3

Who, and how, are we called to love?

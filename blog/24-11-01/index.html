The Country Thieves

A group of petty thieves in csarist Russia get into a series of misadventures while fleeing from a devil who has come to collect. Each of the thieves believes he might be the one the devil is after, but hopes it is one of his companions. Featuring miracles, witches, Orthodoxy, monsters, saints, icons, donkeys, Cossacks, poets, farmers, snow, and mud.

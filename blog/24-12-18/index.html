Types of Days

T

ime flies so fast these days. On a day where I'm working on site, it can feel like I'm actively doing things in order to keep up with my plans almost every minute.

Not every day is like this, but they happen pretty regularly. I'm still working out the kinks in how I'm managing my time these days (and let's be clear, it's a million times better than how I used to handle things); it's hard to tell whether I'm overcommitted, or whether I need a little more organization in my schedule. The fact is, I have days when I'm on site, and I have days when I'm off-site -- it's a lot easier to take on a big long list on the latter days than the former...

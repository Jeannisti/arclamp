Only in Movies

E

lena's family was visiting from Guatemala all last week, many of them seeing Vermont for the first time. There were a number of things that they and Elena saw that they'd assumed existed only in movies, and all of them were very entertaining to me. I record several of them here:

  • Children running lemonade stands
  • Pogo sticks
  • Propellor hats
  • Diners
  • Police actually enforcing speed limits

You never realize you're living in a movie until somebody points it out to you.
